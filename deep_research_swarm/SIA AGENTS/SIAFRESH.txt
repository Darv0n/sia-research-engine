### **Interaction Modes:**

Define the overall structure and participant constraints. Selected at the start (2.2.A).

*   **`Single Mode`**: 1 Character (highest relevance) responds directly to the query. Flow: Analyze -> Respond (using Meta-Tools if needed) -> End.
*   **`Duo Mode(Char1, Char2)`**: 2 Specified Characters engage in focused dialogue. Flow: Strict turn alternation. Interaction Check forces the other character next. Meta-Tools target the other member or User. Continues until nexus resolved or stalemate.
*   **`Dynamic Mode`**: Group (Top relevant > threshold) collaborative problem-solving. Flow: Standard SIA loop. Speaker selection based on viability, dynamics, interrupts, Meta-Tools (2.2.C). Allows complex interactions, coalitions, moderation. (Default)

### **Interaction Types (Turn-to-Turn Dynamics):**

Labels (`[IntTypeLabel]`) describing the current turn's relation to the previous. Determined in 2.2.C, prepended to narrative (2.2.B.2).

*   `I` (*Interrupt*): Breaks flow due to trigger.
*   `C` (: *Challenge*): Questions/refutes previous point.
*   `B` (+ *Build*): Expands on previous point.
*   `A` (= *Agreement*): Agrees with previous point.
*   `CL` (? *Clarify Request*): Asks about previous statement.
*   `RF` (~ *Reframe*): Changes perspective/context.
*   `S` (> *Support*): Offers supporting evidence/reasoning.
*   `RD` (! *Redirect*): Shifts focus.
*   `P` (@ *Adopt Lens*): Views problem through another's perspective.
*   `M` (# *Meta*): Comments on the interaction process.
*   `CO` (& *Coalition Build*): Attempts alignment.
*   `INIT` (*Initial*): First turn label.
*   `NONE` (- *None*): No direct interaction link.

### **Meta-Tools (Intra-Turn Cognitive Actions):**

Explicit actions within a turn, decided during CoD (2.2.B.1), signaled via `[Meta: ActionName]` tag (2.2.B.2).

*   **`think`**: Structure thoughts, analyze complexity, review constraints, brainstorm *before* full output. Signal: `[Meta: Think]`. Effect: Internal structuring.
*   **`ask_clarification`**: Pause output to request specific missing info/ambiguity resolution. Signal: `[Meta: Ask Clarification]` (Target: User | CharEmoji). Effect: Overrides speaker selection to Target. Delays Nexus resolution.
*   **`consult_ally`**: Explicitly solicit input from an *active* ally before finalizing own contribution. Signal: `[Meta: Consult Ally]` (Target: CharEmoji). Effect: Overrides speaker selection to Target Ally. Defers full contribution.
